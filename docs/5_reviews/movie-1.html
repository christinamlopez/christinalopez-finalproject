Meet Me in St. Louis

Vincent Minelli’s “Meet Me In St. Louis” is a movie like no other, a technicolor American dream/love story. Minelli, along with Hollywood starlet and icon Judy Garland, masterfully capture a midwestern life that seems perfect. From the summer music numbers, the suspense of the fall, the whimsy of Christmas and the hope of the new year makes for the perfect movie for our family.
